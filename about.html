About VELOURX

Fashion for every family, every story, every size.

Home›About Us

We started with one idea.

Fashion shouldn't be complicated. It shouldn't require a separate trip to four different stores to dress your family. It shouldn't break the bank. And it definitely shouldn't make you feel like you're not "the right size" or "the right age" to look good.

VELOURX was born in 2021 out of that frustration. We built one brand — one place — where Men, Women, Teens, and Kids can all find their style, in one checkout.

We design everything in-house. We work directly with ethical manufacturers across Portugal, India, and Turkey. We cut out the middlemen. That's how we offer premium quality at prices that actually make sense.

What we stand for.

We believe fashion is personal — it's how you show the world who you are before you even speak. Our job is to give you the tools to do that, beautifully, affordably, and sustainably.

  • Quality First — Every piece is wear-tested before it reaches our store.
  • Inclusive Sizing — XS to XXL across all categories, always.
  • Ethical Production — We audit our factories and publish results annually.
  • Planet Positive — Recyclable packaging across all orders from 2023 onwards.
