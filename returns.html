Returns & Refunds

30-day free returns on every order, no questions asked.

Home›Returns

↩️ 30-day free returns — if you're not 100% happy, we'll make it right. No restocking fees, no drama.

✅ What Can Be Returned

  • Items in original condition — unworn, unwashed
  • Items with all original tags still attached
  • Items in original packaging
  • Items within 30 days of delivery date

❌ What Cannot Be Returned

  • Items marked as "Final Sale"
  • Underwear, swimwear (hygiene reasons)
  • Items that have been worn, washed or damaged
  • Items returned after 30 days

💸 Refund Timeline

Once we receive and inspect your return:

Refund approved: within 3–5 business days
Back to your bank: additional 5–7 business days
Store credit: instant upon approval

🔄 Exchanges

Want a different size or colour? We process exchanges the same as returns — send back your item and place a new order. Your refund will cover the original purchase amount.

📋 How to Start a Return

  • Step 1: Log into your VELOURX account → go to My Orders → click "Return Item"
  • Step 2: Select the item(s) and reason for return
  • Step 3: Print your free prepaid return label (emailed to you instantly)
  • Step 4: Drop the parcel at any post office
  • Step 5: Receive your refund within 3–5 business days of us receiving it
